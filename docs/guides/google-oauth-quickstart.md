# Quick Start: Google OAuth Branding (5 Minutes)

This is the TL;DR version. For detailed instructions, see [GOOGLE_OAUTH_BRANDING_SETUP.md](./GOOGLE_OAUTH_BRANDING_SETUP.md).

---

## What You'll Do

Make the Google sign-in screen show "Heirlooms" and your logo instead of the Supabase URL.

---

## Step 1: Prepare Your Logo (2 min)

- Format: PNG or JPG
- Size: 120px × 120px (square)
- Under 1MB
- Clear and recognizable

---

## Step 2: Configure Branding (2 min)

1. Go to https://console.cloud.google.com/auth/branding
2. Select your Google Cloud project
3. Fill in:
   - **App name**: `Heirlooms`
   - **App logo**: Upload your logo
   - **User support email**: Your support email
   - **Developer contact**: Your email

4. Click **Save**

---

## Step 3: Start Verification (1 min)

1. On the same page, find the **Verification** section
2. Click **Start verification**
3. Follow the prompts
4. Submit your request

**Wait time**: 3-5 business days for Google to review

---

## Important Notes

- Your branding will NOT show until verification is approved
- Users will see "Unverified app" warning until then
- Consider setting up a custom domain for extra trust: https://supabase.com/docs/guides/platform/custom-domains

---

## After Verification Completes

No code changes needed! Your app name and logo will automatically appear on the Google consent screen.

---

## Test It

1. Open your app in incognito mode
2. Click "Continue with Google"
3. You should now see:
   - ✅ "Heirlooms" as the app name
   - ✅ Your logo
   - ✅ Professional appearance
